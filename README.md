# Somewhere — Complete Works

Saga musicale narrative assistée par IA  
(AI-assisted narrative musical saga)

Created by Sébastien Loiret

Initiated on Suno (AI music platform) on January 29, 2025

---

## Overview

Somewhere is a long-form musical storytelling project structured in books and seasons.

- Book 1 (Seasons 1–4) — Complete  
- Book 2 — In progress  

Each song is a chapter in a coherent narrative exploring memory, identity and reality.

### Song 2 — City of Dreamers

[Verse]
In a city safe and bright, behind a towering wall,
Emma dreamed of something more, a world beyond the gate,
Books and stories fed her heart, she longed to change her fate,
Oh, the city's glow felt pale, under night’s distant call.

[Chorus]
She whispered to the moon, her silent prayers of flight,
In the shadows of the streets, she wove tales through the night,
Dreams of hidden lanes and paths, where freedom took its course,
Emma’s spirit danced the wind, with untamed vital force.

[Verse]
In her world of parchment skies, horizons she would chase,
Each adventure in her mind, a spark, an echoed grace,
Streets of cobblestones and sighs, were canvases of dreams,
But the city’s stern embrace, held her in its seams.

[Bridge]
But walls are but a line, a mark upon a map,
Emma knew within her heart, she'd find a secret gap,
Echoes of the grand unknown, called her heart to roam,
In the silence of the night, she’d carve a path to home.

[Verse]
Through the whispers of the trees, the stars would guide her way,
To a world untouched by hands, where her dreams could stay,
The city’s lull did hold her close, but dreams began to swell,
In the corners of her mind, they cast a magic spell.

[Refrain]
So Emma leapt and soared, on wings of hope and light,
Into realms of endless dreams, where day embraced the night,
With a heart as wild as flame, she broke the city’s tie,
In the melody of dreams, beneath the boundless sky.
